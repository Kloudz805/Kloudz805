### 👋 Hi there, I'm Jonny (Kloudz805)

## 🕹️ From Pro-Gaming to Accessible UAS Engineering
I am a UAS Systems Engineer and Open-Source Developer. Many in the community know me as a veteran of the **Evo and Marvel vs. Capcom** pro-gaming scene. I’m taking the exact same "Tournament-Grade" mindset—where every single frame and input counts—and applying it to mission-critical drone technology.

### ♿ Accessibility-First Design & Global Impact
My work goes beyond speed; it’s about **inclusion and reliability**. I specialize in creating cross-compatible systems and custom displays specifically designed for **people with disabilities**, ensuring that high-performance tech is accessible to everyone.

* **Extreme Environment Deployment:** Whether it's white-out snow conditions or high-interference urban zones, I build for "Hard Mode." My systems are designed for **Fast Rapid Deployment** in environments where standard gear fails.
* **The "BenQ" Standard:** I optimize for the highest refresh rates and lowest visual latency (sub-20ms). If you can't see the environment clearly and instantly, the mission is at risk.
* **Wired Reliability:** I treat wireless drone signals like a high-performance braided ethernet cable—zero jitter, zero lag.

### 🚀 Current Projects:
* **Project Leah-SAR:** Open-source Betaflight presets for Search and Rescue. Optimized for rapid deployment in extreme weather (snow/maritime) and high-stress operations.
* **Project Exocoetus (Flying Fish):** Terminal autonomy for signal-denied environments. Ensuring drones stay on mission when the link drops.
* **Accessible Displays:** Developing UI/UX solutions for pilots with disabilities, focusing on high-visibility and cross-platform compatibility.
